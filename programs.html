Our Programs

New Vraja Dham offers regular opportunities for spiritual learning, devotional practice, and community connection. Our programs are open to everyone, whether you are visiting for the first time or have been practicing bhakti-yoga for years.

Weekly Programs

  • Kirtan and Bhajans – Join us for devotional chanting and meditation.
  • Bhagavad-gita Discussion – Explore timeless spiritual teachings in a welcoming setting.
  • Prasadam – Share sanctified vegetarian food with the community.
  • Association – Spend time with devotees and guests in a positive spiritual atmosphere.

Special Events

Throughout the year, we also celebrate major Vaishnava festivals and special gatherings. Please check back for updates as the community grows.
