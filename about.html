About New Vraja Dham

New Vraja Dham is an ISKCON temple and spiritual community based in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, established in 2025. It is dedicated to sharing the teachings of bhakti-yoga, the path of devotional service, as presented in the Gaudiya Vaishnava tradition.

Our mission is to create a welcoming and uplifting environment where individuals can explore spiritual life through chanting, study, and service. We regularly host kirtan (devotional music), classes on sacred texts such as the Bhagavad-gita and Srimad Bhagavatam, and community gatherings centered around prasadam (sanctified vegetarian meals).

What is ISKCON?

The International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON) was founded in 1966 by His Divine Grace A. C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada. ISKCON aims to promote the well-being of society by teaching the science of Krishna consciousness, helping individuals reconnect with their spiritual nature and develop a deeper relationship with God.

What is Bhakti-Yoga?

Bhakti-yoga is the process of developing a loving relationship with God through devotional practices such as chanting, hearing spiritual knowledge, and engaging in selfless service. It is a joyful and practical path that can be integrated into daily life, regardless of one’s background or experience.

Everyone is Welcome

Whether you are completely new to spirituality or have been practicing for years, you are warmly invited to visit New Vraja Dham. Our goal is to provide a space where everyone can feel comfortable, ask questions, and grow at their own pace.
