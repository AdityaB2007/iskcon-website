About New Vraja Dham

New Vraja Dham is a growing spiritual community in Baton Rouge dedicated to sharing bhakti-yoga through kirtan, philosophy, prasadam, and devotional service.

Our programs are centered around spiritual growth, meaningful association, and creating a welcoming atmosphere where visitors can explore Krishna consciousness at their own pace.

About ISKCON

The International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON), commonly known as the Hare Krishna movement, is a worldwide organization dedicated to the practice of Bhakti-yoga (devotional service to Lord Krishna).

Founded in New York City in 1966 by His Divine Grace A. C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Srila Prabhupada, its teachings are rooted in ancient Vedic texts like the Bhagavad Gita and Bhagavata Purana.

About Srila Prabhupada

Srila Prabhupada was a visionary spiritual teacher and the founding-acharya of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON), who single-handedly built a global multi-racial community of devotees and revitalized ancient Gaudiya Vaishnavism across six continents.

Despite starting his mission at an advanced age with minimal resources, he translated dozens of monumental Vedic texts into English, established over a hundred temples, and permanently transformed the global landscape of modern spirituality and yoga culture, embodying devotion, humility, surrender to Lord Krishna, and compassionate outreach.

What is Bhakti-Yoga?

Bhakti-yoga is the process of developing a loving relationship with Krishna through chanting, hearing spiritual wisdom, devotional service, and remembrance.

It is a practical and joyful spiritual path that can be integrated into daily life regardless of one's background and level of experience.
