Chanting and Japa Meditation


What is Chanting?


Chanting the Hare Krishna maha-mantra is a central practice of bhakti-yoga. It helps focus the mind, purify the heart, and deepen remembrance of God (Krishna).


This mantra is:
Hare Krishna, Hare Krishna, Krishna Krishna, Hare Hare / Hare Rama, Hara Rama, Rama Rama, Hare Hare


Japa Meditation


Japa is the personal chanting of the Hare Krishna maha-mantra on beads. Many practitioners chant daily as part of their spiritual vows and practices.

Kirtan and Musical Meditation


What is Kirtan?


Kirtan refers to congregational chanting of Lord Krishna's holy names, often accompanied by Indian classical instruments such as the harmonium, kartals, and mridangam.
